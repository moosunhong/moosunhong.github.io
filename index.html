    • Our research is in applied mathematics and control theory and its application to manufacturing systems where the control of events at the molecular scale is key to product quality.

    Some specific applications include:

    • Mechanistic modeling, data analytics, and optimal design control of continuous platforms for monoclonal antibody, vaccine, and gene therapy manufacturing;
    • Machine learning and multiscale modeling methods for early prediction and accelerated design of lithium-ion batteries;
    • Mechanistic modeling, design, and control of pharmaceutical, protein, and polymer crystallization processes.
